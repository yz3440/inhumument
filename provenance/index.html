Provenance & License#

The book#

A Human Document by W. H. Mallock — the one-volume Chapman & Hall 1892 "New Edition", the edition Tom Phillips treated to make A Humument. Page numbers map one-to-one onto A Humument, so a page here is the printed book page (= the A Humument page), 1–367.

The source scan is Internet Archive item ahumandocumenta04mallgoog, digitized by Google from a public-domain copy. The OCR (Apple Vision) and the CV pipeline that produced the dataset live in the humument repo; the data is published as the humument-data and humument-images npm packages.

The packages#

  • humument — renderer-agnostic core.
  • humument-data / humument-images — the dataset.
  • inhumument-lib — the p5 rendering layer (this project).
